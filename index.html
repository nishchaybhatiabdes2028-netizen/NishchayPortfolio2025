DESCRIPTION

"There Was a Tree" is an invitation to confront the recesses within our thoughts and to explore the short-lived and confusing nature of dreams. The project hopes to encourage contemplation by blurring the lines between dream and reality and capturing the essence of a restless night in a sequence of photographs.

ABOUT ME

I'm Nishchay, a multi-disciplinary designer from Delhi, now based in Mumbai, currently studying UI/UX at ATLAS SkillTech University. My work spans UI/UX, visual identity, and photography/video shaping digital experiences and brand stories through clear, user-focused design.

I've collaborated with organizations like Anon Global Foundation, Gryd Co, and Aurora Creatives, and also led student initiatives with XINO and DPS Rohini.

Beyond the screen, I enjoy capturing new places through my lens, blending design and storytelling into everything I do.
